About Us.

We are a family run business formed in September 2009 based in the Swindon town centre, we are ideally situated to provide the best dry cleaning services to the local area.

Today we are recognised Swindon best Dry Cleaners by our customers.. Our success is based on two main factors: loyalty and feedback from our customers. We work with a passion to deliver astounding customer service, quality. However, the most important factor is our non-stop commitment to invest in training and cutting-edge eco-friendly technology.


Our aim is to serve the local community with a friendly and sensibly priced service within the dry cleaning, laundry and tailoring fields. We offer our customers the highest level of service, be they domestic or commercial, and we take pride in our quality service that is tailored to meet customer needs. Attention to detail is important to us, as is your trust and ensuring your garments are treated with the utmost respect. You can rest assured you will receive what you’re looking for from us.


We welcome you to pop in and visit us, or to give us a call on 01793 617307 and we will make sure to accommodate you and answer any questions you might have.
